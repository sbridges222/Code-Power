Understanding Implicit Bias

Implicit bias refers to the unconscious attitudes and stereotypes that influence our perceptions, decisions, and behaviors, often in ways that disadvantage certain groups.

Assess your own implicit biases with an interactive quiz that uses the Implicit Association Test (IAT) methodology. You can select from different categories like race, gender, age, and more to explore your unconscious associations.

Intersectionality in Action

Intersectionality is a framework for understanding how an individual's overlapping identities (such as race, gender, class, and disability) intersect to create unique experiences of privilege and marginalization.

Watch this video on how privilege and systemic inequalities can impact people's opportunities and outcomes, even in a simple race for a cash prize.
